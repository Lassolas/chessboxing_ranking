Chessboxing skill evaluator

Choose Fighter A's chess and boxing levels by scrolling the lists below. Each cell then shows Fighter A's probability of winning a chessboxing match against an opponent of that skill, on a red→yellow→green color scale. Touch and drag on the grid to inspect opponents on mobile; on desktop, press and drag to inspect. The black-outlined band marks opponents that win against Fighter A ≈ 50 % of the time — the fair-fight zone.

Win probability model (fitted from simulation data, RMSE ≈ 0.026): P(win) = σ( 2·tanh(Δchess / 3) + Δboxing ) with Δ = Fighter A − opponent.

Chessboxing rating (★) is an absolute rank: each cell's average win probability against every other cell, normalized to [0,1] across the grid and shown as 0–5 stars.
